North Star — Morning Return Rate (MRR)
% of weekly active users who complete the daily crossword during the 6:00–10:00 AM window, on consecutive days.
Behavioral Layer

Replaces generic "Come solve today's puzzle!" alerts with hyper-local, curiosity-driven teasers tied to shared cultural memory — triggering dopamine before the app even opens.

Product Loop Layer

"Streak Rescue" mercy infrastructure — a broken streak unlocks a recovery micro-puzzle instead of a hard reset, removing the punitive exit door.

Growth Metric Layer

Builds baseline DAU by converting erratic, campaign-driven opens into a predictable daily morning routine.

Reset-to-Zero Burnout

A single missed day resets streaks to absolute zero — a heavy psychological penalty that causes immediate, permanent churn.

Banner Blindness

Generic alerts like "Your daily crossword is ready!" are ignored — we lose the user's first 15 minutes of morning attention.

Transactional Exit

Once solved, the grid turns green and the session simply ends — no lingering reward, no bridge to tomorrow.
